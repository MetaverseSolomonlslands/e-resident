INTRODUCTION OF SOLOMON

ISLANDS-COUNTRY INTRODUCTION

The Solomon Islands is an island country located in the southwestern of South Pacific (5°-12°South latitude, 155°-170°East longitude) A member of the The British Commonwealth of Nations. The capital Honiara.On September 21, 2019, China and Solomon Islands established diplomatic relations

There are more than 990 islands in the country, with a total land area of 28,450 square kilometers, a marine exclusive economic zone of 1.6 million square kilometers, and the forest area occupies 90% of the country.

Authorized by the Central Provincial Government of Solomon , people can develop private islands , and Solomon will provide green card passports for those who purchase private islands.

Solomon citizens can enter 131 countries around the world visa-free.
